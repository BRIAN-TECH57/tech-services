Pro Tips: Protect Your Hardware

Follow these expert tips to keep your PC running at peak performance:

1. Keep it Cool

Always use your laptop on a hard, flat surface. Using it on a bed or sofa blocks airflow, causing the processor to overheat and slow down.

2. Surge Protection

Voltage spikes are common. Always plug your expensive electronics into a surge protector to prevent motherboard failure.

3. Clean Your Files

Run a "Disk Cleanup" once a month to remove temporary files that slow down your system boot time.
