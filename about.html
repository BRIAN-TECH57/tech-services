💻 PC Maintenance Tips

1. Surge Protection

Voltage spikes are common in Meru. Protect your motherboard by using a surge protector or UPS.

2. Airflow Matters

Keep your PC on a hard surface. Beddings block fans, leading to overheating and slow performance.

3. Professional Servicing

Have your laptop opened and cleaned once a year to remove dust and replace thermal paste.
